Bus Discipline & Guidelines

  • Maintain discipline and proper behavior while using university bus services
  • Do not disturb the peaceful environment inside the bus
  • Keep the bus clean and avoid littering
  • Be respectful to fellow students, faculty members, and bus staff
  • Students are requested to maintain proper dress code and professional behavior
  • Violation of these guidelines may result in suspension of bus services
